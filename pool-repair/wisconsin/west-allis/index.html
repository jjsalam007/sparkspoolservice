Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the pool's surface to remove leaves, particles, and bugs. Use a pool vacuum for consistent cleaning the pool's bottom, and scrub the pool walls and tiles to thwart the growth of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Regularly, follow the manufacturer's recommendations to clean or replace the filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. This maintenance ensures optimal water circulation by ensuring the filter is free of debris.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Always keep in mind to perform regular water testing and adjust the necessary chemical levels to sustain proper pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. Doing so is essential for preventing the development of algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

Consistently, apply a shock treatment the pool with either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This practice is crucial for eliminating any resistant bacteria and algae to regular chlorination methods.
