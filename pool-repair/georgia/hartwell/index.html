Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the water surface to eliminate leaves, debris, and insects. Use a vacuum cleaner designed for pools for consistent cleaning the pool's bottom, and brush the walls and tiles to thwart the formation of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Regularly, adhere to the manufacturer's guidelines to maintain or replace your filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. This maintenance ensures effective water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Always keep in mind to perform regular water testing and adjust the necessary chemical levels to sustain proper pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. This practice is essential for preventing the development of algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

On a regular basis, administer a treatment to shock the pool using either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This step is essential to eradicate any resistant bacteria and algae to regular chlorination methods.
