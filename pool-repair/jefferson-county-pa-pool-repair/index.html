Regular Cleaning

Maintain Water Chemistry

Always keep in mind to test the water consistently and adjust the necessary chemical levels to maintain the correct pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. This practice is essential for staving off algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

On a regular basis, administer a treatment to shock the pool with either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This step is essential to eradicate any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to the typical chlorination.
