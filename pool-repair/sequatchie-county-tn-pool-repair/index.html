Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the water surface to eliminate leaves, particles, and insects. Employ a pool vacuum for consistent cleaning the bottom of the pool, as well as scrub the pool walls and tiles to prevent the formation of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Regularly, adhere to the manufacturer's guidelines to maintain or replace your filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. This maintenance guarantees effective water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Don't forget to perform regular water testing and make necessary adjustments to sustain proper pH, alkalinity, as well as chlorine/bromine levels. This practice plays a crucial role in staving off algae as well as bacteria.

Shock Treatment

Consistently, apply a treatment to shock the pool using either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This practice is crucial to eradicate any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to regular chlorination methods.
