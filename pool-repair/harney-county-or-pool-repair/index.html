Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the water surface to eliminate leaves, particles, and bugs. Employ a vacuum cleaner designed for pools to regularly clean the bottom of the pool, and scrub the pool walls and tiles to prevent the formation of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Periodically, adhere to the manufacturer's guidelines to maintain or replace your filter cartridge, or perform backwashing for sand or DE filters. This maintenance ensures effective water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Don't forget to test the water consistently and make necessary adjustments to sustain the correct pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. This practice is essential for preventing the development of algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

Consistently, administer a treatment to shock the pool using either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This step is essential for eliminating any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to regular chlorination methods.
