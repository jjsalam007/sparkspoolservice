Regular Cleaning

Regularly, adhere to the manufacturer's guidelines to clean or replace the filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. These actions guarantees effective water circulation by ensuring the filter is free of debris.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Always keep in mind to perform regular water testing and make necessary adjustments to sustain proper pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. Doing so is essential for preventing the development of algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

Consistently, administer a shock treatment the pool with either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This step is essential for eliminating any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to the typical chlorination.
