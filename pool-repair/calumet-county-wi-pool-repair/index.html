Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the pool's surface to remove leaves, particles, and insects. Use a pool vacuum for consistent cleaning the bottom of the pool, and scrub the pool walls and tiles to prevent the growth of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Periodically, follow the manufacturer's recommendations to maintain or replace your filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. These actions ensures optimal water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Don't forget to test the water consistently and adjust the necessary chemical levels to maintain proper pH, alkalinity, and chlorine/bromine levels. Doing so is essential for preventing the development of algae and bacteria.

Shock Treatment

Consistently, administer a shock treatment the pool with either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This practice is crucial to eradicate any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to the typical chlorination.
