Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

On a daily basis, remember to skim the pool's surface to remove leaves, particles, and bugs. Use a pool vacuum to regularly clean the pool's bottom, as well as brush the walls and tiles to prevent the formation of algae.

Periodically, follow the manufacturer's recommendations to clean or replace the filter cartridge, or perform backwashing for sand or DE filters. This maintenance guarantees optimal water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Don't forget to test the water consistently and adjust the necessary chemical levels to maintain the correct pH, alkalinity, as well as chlorine/bromine levels. This practice is essential for staving off algae as well as bacteria.

Shock Treatment

On a regular basis, administer a treatment to shock the pool using either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This step is essential to eradicate any resistant bacteria and algae to the typical chlorination.
