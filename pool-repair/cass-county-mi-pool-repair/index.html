Pool Maintenance Tips

Regular Cleaning

Each day, make it a habit to clear the water surface to eliminate leaves, particles, and insects. Employ a vacuum cleaner designed for pools for consistent cleaning the bottom of the pool, as well as scrub the walls and tiles to thwart the growth of algae.

Check Pump and Motor

Regularly, follow the manufacturer's recommendations to maintain or replace your filter cartridge, or backwash the sand or DE filter. This maintenance ensures effective water circulation by keeping the filter clean.

Maintain Water Chemistry

Always keep in mind to perform regular water testing and make necessary adjustments to maintain the correct pH, alkalinity, as well as chlorine/bromine levels. This practice is essential for preventing the development of algae as well as bacteria.

Shock Treatment

On a regular basis, apply a treatment to shock the pool with either chlorine or a non-chlorine option. This practice is crucial for eliminating any algae and bacteria that may be resistant to the typical chlorination.
